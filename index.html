How It Works

CGLINK is quite easy and intuitive to use.

Whilst working in Google Docs, Slides and Sheets, users can directly start a conversation with anyone who has the right to edit or comment on the file they are reviewing. They do not need to copy contacts from Google and search/paste them in Jabber anymore, but can now contact others straight from the Google application, using all functionalities that Cisco Jabber offers such as, for example, chat, call, user’s presence and desktop sharing.

All these functionalities are also available in Gmail and Google Contacts, from where users can directly start a chat or call via a Gmail hover card using Cisco Jabber.

Why CGLINK?

Collaborating with colleagues, customers and partners nowadays is easier than ever before thanks to the great diversity of online collaboration tools. However, this same diversity often causes interruptions in the workflow. For example, the constant switching between Google products such as Gmail, Sheets, Docs, Slides etc. and Cisco tools such as Jabber costs additional time and may lead to inefficient team communication or even to information loss.

CGLINK is a browser plugin for desktop that enables users to communicate easily from within all Google applications and empowers them to collaborate contextually within the documents, presentations, data sheets, emails and contact lists.

CGlink runs on your existing infrastructure and doesn’t require additional investments in hardware.

CGLINK allows users to:

  • • integrate Jabber in Google applications such as Gmail, Docs, Sheets and Slides
  • • start a chat from a Gmail hover card using Cisco Jabber
  • • start a call from a Gmail hover card using Cisco Jabber
  • • start a group conversation from an e-mail in Gmail or a document in Docs
  • • start contextual collaboration with Jabber contacts directly from a Google document
  • • view other users Jabbers presence in Gmail hover card

These functionalities save your employees time and minimize their efforts, allowing them to focus on their core activities and increase their efficiency. Moreover, the app is easy to use and easy to install.

To sum up, CGLINK gathers everything you needed for efficient team collaboration in one place, ensuring seamless integration between the apps in GSuite and the Cisco collaboration tools.
