Why Iriana?

Most of the outbound calling is routine - reminders and notifications, alerts and standard offers. Iriana saves you time and money, avoiding the repeatable and boring task of calling everyone in your list individually. Iriana is your personal automated call center or a powerful tool for your marketing team. It helps you keep your customers close and listen to what they have to say.

Now you can build your campaigns and automate the whole process. Our cloud based NLU powered platform will call and engage with your users and ask for their response according to your conversation logic. Responses will be recorded and available for analyses or export.

Use fewer call agents as up to 90% of the calls will be served without the need of human interaction. Natural language understanding significantly changed the way you can do it.

Call analytics lets you measure the drop rate and find the weak points in your flow. As a result, you can optimize and increase performance.

Integrate our API directly with your ERP to align your client's preferences with your business flow.
